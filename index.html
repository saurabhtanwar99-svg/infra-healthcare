Professional Medical Staffing

Infra-Healthcare is a premier provider of qualified health professionals across the National Capital Region. Our staffing division specializes in delivering high-caliber talent for adult care and midwifery, including specialized Nursing staff, Doctors, Pharmacists, and General Duty Assistants (GDA). We understand that the backbone of any medical institution is its human capital. Therefore, we employ a rigorous international screening process to ensure every professional we place meets global clinical standards. Whether you are looking for temporary staffing to handle peak loads or permanent specialist placements, our team provides seamless integration into your existing hospital workflows.

Nursing & Midwifery

We supply highly trained nurses and midwives who specialize in maternal health and adult clinical care. Our professionals are trained in the latest patient-handling protocols and emergency response, ensuring your facility maintains the highest safety ratings.

Physicians & Specialists

Access a network of qualified Doctors and Pharmacists. Our specialists are vetted for their clinical expertise and their ability to work within high-pressure environments like ICU and Emergency departments.

Support Staff (GDA)

General Duty Assistants and pharmacists are critical to hospital operations. We provide reliable, well-trained support staff who assist in patient mobility, sanitation, and pharmaceutical management to ensure smooth daily operations.
